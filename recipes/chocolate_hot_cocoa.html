Thor's Dark Chocolate Hot Cocoa

Enjoy Thor's favorite Hot Cocoa, you just need a few ingredients and a little bit of time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons brown sugar, or to taste
  • 2 ounces dark chocolate, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon heavy whipping cream, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan over medium heat until just before boiling, 3 to 4 minutes. Add brown sugar and stir until dissolved, about 1 minute. Stir dark chocolate into milk until melted, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat and stir cream and cinnamon into milk mixture.
